Core Features for HomeLab Enthusiasts

We focus on providing the purest and most direct management experience, allowing you to focus on your applications, not tedious operations.

Cluster Management

Easily import and switch between multiple kubeconfigs, whether by pasting text or importing a file.

Workload Insights

Intuitively view the status of Deployments, StatefulSets, DaemonSets, and other resources at a glance.

Live Logs & Terminal

View real-time logs and `exec` into Pod containers directly on your mobile device. Troubleshooting has never been easier.

Easy Resource Control

Quickly scale replicas, restart Pods, and delete resources. All frequent operations are designed to be at your fingertips.
