Rediscover Your Precious Memories

Displayable transforms your iPhone and iPad into a powerful, highly customizable digital photo frame, designed specifically to connect to your self-hosted Immich server.

Requires iOS 16.0 or later

App Screenshots

A glimpse into the beautiful and intuitive interface of Displayable.

Core Features

Connect to Immich

Securely connect to your self-hosted Immich instance using your server address and API key. Your data remains private.

Powerful Filtering

Create the perfect slideshow by filtering by person, one or more albums, or a custom date range.

Dynamic Layouts

Choose between Fit, Fill, and an intelligent Collage mode that smartly arranges portrait and landscape photos.

Animated Effects

A subtle Ken Burns effect adds gentle panning and zooming to your photos, making your slideshow more dynamic.

Rich Photo Info

Optionally display an elegant overlay with detailed EXIF data and the location where the photo was taken.
Coming soon.

Native & Private

Built with SwiftUI for a fast and fluid experience. Your photos and server info are never sent to any third party.
